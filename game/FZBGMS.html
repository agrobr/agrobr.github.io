You'll feel the adrenaline as you help Santa deliver his gifts while leaping across treacherous gaps and maneuvering through deadly obstacles. </p> <h2> How to Play </h2> <p> Playing Santabalt is all about timing and reflexes. Your task is simply to <b>tap or click</b> on the screen at the right time for Santa to jump.
