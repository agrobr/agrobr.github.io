Floppy Pipe

<h2> Description </h2> <p> Play as a flying pipe in <b>Floppy Pipe</b>, a thrilling twist on the popular game "Flappy Bird". In this game, you have to dodge a flock of different chickens to survive. Each chicken presents a unique <b>challenge</b> and a new opportunity to rack up points. The longer you last, the higher your score, making each new round an exciting race against time. </p> <h2> How to play </h2> <p> To navigate your pipe, simply click or tap anywhere on the screen. The <b>objective</b> is to keep the pipe aloft and avoid hitting any of the chickens. Remember, the further you go without collision, the higher your score! So aim for maximum distance with flawless <b>flying skills.</b> </p> <h2> Tips and Tricks </h2> <p> Timing is everything in Floppy Pipe. To build a high score quickly, maintain a steady rhythm and try to anticipate where the next chicken will appear. Be sure not to fly too high or low - staying in the middle can help avoid unexpected obstacles. </p> <h2> Features </h2> <ul> <li> <b>Detailed pixel art:</b> Enjoy beautifully-crafted pixelated chickens and backgrounds. </li> <li> <b>Addictive gameplay:</b> The simple yet challenging mechanics make this a game you'll keep coming back to. </li> <li> <b>Friendly competition:</b> Try to beat your high score or compete with friends for bragging rights. </li> </ul>
